## 5. CONSIDERATION AND PAYMENT TERMS

5.1 **Purchase Price:** In consideration for the transfer of the Licensed Technology, Licensee shall pay to Licensor the sum of **$5,000,000.00** (Five Million United States Dollars), payable as follows:

(a) **Initial Payment:** $2,500,000.00 due upon execution of this Agreement;
(b) **Second Payment:** $1,500,000.00 due within thirty (30) days of the Transfer Date;
(c) **Final Payment:** $1,000,000.00 due within ninety (90) days of the Transfer Date.

5.2 **Additional Royalties:** Licensee shall pay Licensor royalties equal to 3% of Net Revenues derived from the Licensed Technology for a period of five (5) years following the Transfer Date, payable quarterly within thirty (30) days following the end of each calendar quarter.

5.3 **Payment Method:** All payments shall be made by wire transfer of immediately available funds to such bank account as designated by Licensor in writing.

5.4 **Late Payment:** Any payment not made when due shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month (or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less) from the date due until paid.
