**EFFECTIVE DATE:** January 15, 2024

**PARTIES INVOLVED:**
- **BUYER:** TechCore Solutions, Inc., a corporation duly organized and existing under the laws of Delaware, with principal offices at 1500 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, California 94105

## 1. RECITALS

WHEREAS, Buyer is engaged in the business of developing, marketing, and licensing enterprise software solutions, and has an established presence in markets that include technology services, cloud-based platforms, and digital transformation solutions;

WHEREAS, Seller owns and operates certain proprietary technology, software products, intellectual property, customer relationships, and business assets that are complementary to Buyer’s existing offerings and strategic growth objectives;

WHEREAS, Buyer desires to acquire, and Seller desires to sell, the Business, the Assets, and the equity interests of Seller, subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement;

## 3. PURCHASE AND SALE

3.1 **Purchase and Sale of Equity Interests.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, at the Closing, Seller shall sell, transfer, convey, assign, and deliver to Buyer, and Buyer shall purchase and acquire from Seller, all of the outstanding equity interests of GreenCode Systems LLC (the "Equity Interests"), free and clear of all Encumbrances except Permitted Encumbrances.

3.2 **Purchase Price.** The aggregate Purchase Price for the Acquisition shall be Fifty-Six Million United States Dollars ($56,000,000.00), subject to adjustment in accordance with Section 8.

3.3 **Allocation of Purchase Price.** The Purchase Price shall be allocated among the Assets for Tax purposes in accordance with Article 8 and in a manner reasonably acceptable to both Parties.

3.4 **Assets.** In addition to the Equity Interests, Buyer shall acquire all rights, title, and interest in the Assets of Seller that constitute the Business, as set forth on the Asset Schedule.

3.5 **Assumed Liabilities.** Buyer shall assume only those liabilities and obligations expressly set forth in Section 5.3 (the "Assumed Liabilities").

3.6 **Excluded Liabilities.** Seller shall retain all liabilities and obligations not expressly assumed by Buyer, including those described in Section 5.4 (the "Excluded Liabilities").

## 4. PURCHASE PRICE AND PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 **Purchase Price Components.** The Purchase Price shall consist of:

(a) cash consideration of Forty Million United States Dollars ($40,000,000.00);
(b) the issuance of equity consideration representing sixteen million United States Dollars ($16,000,000.00) in equity value of Buyer to holders of the Equity Interests, subject to the terms of the Equity Issuance Agreement attached as Schedule 4;
(c) the assumption of the Assumed Liabilities.

4.2 **Payment at Closing.** At the Closing, Buyer shall pay the cash portion of the Purchase Price by wire transfer of immediately available funds to an account designated by Seller, and shall deliver the equity consideration in accordance with the Equity Issuance Agreement.

4.3 **Escrow.** A portion of the cash consideration in the amount of Five Million United States Dollars ($5,000,000.00) shall be deposited into an escrow account to secure Seller’s indemnification obligations under Article 14 (the "Escrow Amount"). The terms of the escrow shall be set forth in the Escrow Agreement attached as Schedule 5.

4.4 **Working Capital Adjustment.** The Purchase Price shall be adjusted by the Working Capital Adjustment in accordance with Section 8.

4.5 **Post-Closing Payment.** If, after the Closing, a calculation pursuant to Section 8 results in an additional payment due from Buyer to Seller or a refund due from Seller to Buyer, such payment or refund shall be made within thirty (30) days after the parties agree to the final adjustment calculation.

4.6 **Payment Withholding.** Buyer may withhold from any amounts otherwise payable to Seller amounts required by applicable law, including taxes, liens, or other deductions, provided Buyer promptly notifies Seller of any such withholding.

4.7 **Currency.** All amounts payable under this Agreement shall be denominated in and paid in lawful money of the United States of America.

## 5. ASSUMPTIONS AND EXCLUSIONS

5.1 **Assumed Liabilities.** Buyer shall assume and agree to discharge the following liabilities of Seller as of the Closing Date:

(a) liabilities arising out of the operation of the Business after the Closing Date;
(b) liabilities under contracts and agreements that are assigned to Buyer at the Closing;
(c) accrued wages, salaries, and benefits of employees for services performed on or after the Closing Date;
(d) liabilities arising from warranties provided to customers for products and services delivered after the Closing Date;
(e) obligations to maintain insurance for the Business after the Closing Date.

5.2 **Assignment of Contracts.** Buyer shall assume Seller’s obligations under the Contracts listed in Schedule 6, subject to approval by the counterparty and compliance with applicable assignment provisions.

5.3 **Excluded Liabilities.** Seller shall retain and be responsible for the following liabilities, whether accrued, known, unknown, contingent, or otherwise:

(a) liabilities related to Seller’s operation prior to the Closing Date, including any trade payables and accrued expenses;
(b) liabilities arising from any breach of representations, warranties, or covenants made by Seller in this Agreement;
(c) liabilities under Contracts not assigned to Buyer;
(d) liabilities for Taxes based on periods ending on or prior to the Closing Date;
(e) liabilities associated with employee claims, severance obligations, or benefits earned prior to the Closing Date, except as otherwise assumed in writing by Buyer;
(f) environmental liabilities and remediation obligations arising prior to the Closing Date.

5.4 **Excluded Assets.** Seller shall retain the following assets, which are excluded from the Acquisition:

(a) cash and cash equivalents not included in the Target Net Working Capital;
(b) Seller’s tax refunds, prepaid expenses, and refunds of Taxes attributable to periods prior to the Closing Date;
(c) employee records and other administrative records unrelated to the Business, except as necessary for Buyer to operate the Business after the Closing Date;
(d) assets of Seller that are expressly excluded in the Asset Schedule.

5.5 **Third-Party Consents.** Seller shall obtain all necessary consents to transfer or assign Contracts, licenses, permits, or Intellectual Property as required for the Acquisition.
